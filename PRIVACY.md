# Privacy Policy

**Sidenotes V2** is a browser sidebar extension for Opera.

## Data Collection

Sidenotes does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. There are no analytics, no tracking, no external requests, and no servers involved.

## Data Storage

All notes, folders, and preferences are stored exclusively on your local device using the browser's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device.

## Third Parties

No third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are used. No data is shared with any third party.

## Contact

If you have any questions, open an issue at the project repository.
